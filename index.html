Welcome to the Hackathon

Join us for a collaborative, hands-on hackathon where students will test, evaluate, and redesign a national civic tech platform. Compete for cash, gear, and recognition while solving real-world problems.

We're teaming up with All Americans Vote for an event that blends creativity, cybersecurity, and UI/UX. Work in teams, learn from experts, and build something meaningful.

Objectives of the Hackathon

  • Redesign for Usability: Improve platform design for accessibility and clarity.
  • Audit for Security: Identify vulnerabilities and risks.
  • Test for Load Performance: Evaluate performance under pressure.
  • Present Actionable Solutions: Submit findings with visuals and recommendations.

Why Participate?

This isn’t just any hackathon—it's your chance to impact a civic tech platform while gaining real-world experience in cybersecurity, performance testing, and UI/UX design.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Who can participate? All VSU students, all majors welcome.

Do I need a team? Yes, but we can help connect you if needed.

Max team size? Up to 4 members.

New to hackathons? No worries! This is a learning opportunity.

Is it virtual or in-person? Hybrid: virtual early in the week, in-person Thursday & Friday.

Meals? Yes—meals and snacks provided during in-person sessions.

What to bring? Laptop, headphones, comfy clothes, and good vibes!
